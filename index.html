Q&A Section

Is this only for millennials?
Nope. The only "millennial" influence on this project is the way in which this is delivered and created. Us, millennials, have a short focus and want the learning to be fun and engaging, therefore, the lessons are sharp, short and fun. But the approach and the proprietary programme can help anyone regardless of their age or stage in life.
What will I get as part of this deal?
You will get access to the whole 5+ hs course, an audio version of it that you can listen anywhere, a 45 MIN CAREER COACHING SESSION at the end of the programme to optimise your career plan, access to career coaches and mentors throughout the programme, a ton of resources and exercises gathered and curated especially for the purpose of this course plus a Personal Purpose Workbook (online - where you keep all your notes and reflections).
What happens if I cannot find my purpose immediately? Do people really find their life mission in 10 days?
Some people find it after less than five days. Some people find it a week from finishing the programme while walking in the park and reflecting on the programme. Each of us has their unique clock and you don't have to rush it. It's not a race and we don't encourage this. The more you put into this the easier you will reach your breakthrough. If you're not happy with the programme you will get your money back (although none of our customers has requested this in the past).
How is this different from the rest of the programmes?
SICS is different because it doesn’t just promote working on purpose it provides a clear path to help you identify what that purpose is.
Do you help with the CV?
We usually refer people to some of the really good sites on the internet for CV assistance. After MH you will have a career that you love, feel fulfilled in, and, more importantly, you will be helping the world evolve positively in your own unique fashion.
What if my English is not so good?
We are looking to translate the programme into 3 main languages, based on people's vote after Kickstarter campaign: we will translate the subtitles and the workbook, which you can download on your PC.
