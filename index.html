The Course

The online experience is made out of 10 modules or steps as we'd like to call them. Each step contains a number of microlessons and exercises that are up to 30 minutes all together so you can take each step in a different day OR ALL AT ONCE although we don't recommend this approach.

You are not alone!

You will be in touch with people that already managed the course and want to give back. Learn the stories about how this programme had changed their life making them successful and with purpose

Learning By Doing

Simple yet powerful visualisation exercises to help you get to the core of who you really are Insightful exercises that you can do in front of your PC or with a friend/member of your family. We put a lot of work to ensure this isn't another online course and designed the programme using principles of neuroscience that just feels light and easily digestible. It's not a course or an online programme, but an experience.
